The world was once engulfed in darkness, demons and dark spirits roamed the lands life was unbearable and diseased the people were losing hope and nearly succumbed to the darkness. All hope seemed lost. It was an act of divine intervention that the sun rose like a pheonix rising from the ashes cleansing the lands and driving hope once again, promoting the growing of life and getting rid sickness and banishing the dark entities back to their holes.

Life is a precious thing that shouldn't be wasted or thrown away. Much can be accomplished in just one life time and your memory will live on in the generations to come. Life is like the droplets of rain purified by an intense storm that drop to the earth below to nurture life. Remember it is always the darkest and coldest before the break of dawn.

"Keep your face always toward the sun and the shadows shall fall behind you."
