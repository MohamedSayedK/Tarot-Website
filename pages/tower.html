A tall spire perched atop the mountain is shown on the Tower card. The tower is a massive obstruct standing tall imposing upon you housing all of your insecurities and fears. The tower is the construct and entity of your shortcomings that you try to escape from and turn your back to, but no matter how far you run or turn a blind eye to it will always be in your consciousness like a dark cloud and will always shadow over you. You want to get away from the chaos and carnage within. The Tower is a representation of ambition that is built on false foundations. In order to make room for something new and throw out the old, the tower must be destroyed. Its insights may appear at a moment of insight or inspiration.

"In our consciousness, there are many negative seeds and also many positive seeds. The practice is to avoid watering the negative seeds, and to identify the water the positive seeds every day."
